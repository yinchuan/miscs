# fedora-18
## mplayer
1. 先加rpmfusion源
2. 安装libcaca.rpm [下载][1]
3. yum install gnome-mplayer

[1]: http://apt.sw.be/redhat/el6/en/x86_64/rpmforge/RPMS/libcaca-0.99-0.1.beta17.el6.rf.x86_64.rpm
## ibus-wubi
1. 安装ibus-table-chinese-wubi-jidian-1.4.6-1.fc18.noarch
2. 安装gnome-tweak-tool-3.6.1-2.fc18.noarch
3. 用gnome-tweak-tool打开所有的输入源
4. 在system-settings里添加中文输入源
